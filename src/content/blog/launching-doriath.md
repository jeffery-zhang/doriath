This site starts with a simple premise: the personal website should be easier to maintain
than the accounts orbiting it.

Astro gives the project a static output, content collections keep Markdown structured, and
GitHub Pages makes deployment cheap enough that the publishing habit can matter more than
the hosting bill.

The goal is not novelty. The goal is a quiet home for writing that stays readable years
from now.
